## Executive Summary

**Market Landscape:** The construction project management software market is valued at $9.87 billion (2024) and projected to reach $21.04 billion by 2032. 35% of contractors plan to invest in PM tools in 2025.

**Key Insight:** There's a significant gap between enterprise solutions ($250-400+/month) and basic tools. Small-to-medium contractors (like Kevin's target market) are underserved by current offerings.

**Opportunity for ProjectTrackr:** Position as the affordable, simple alternative for small masonry restoration contractors who don't need enterprise complexity.

**Best for:** Small-to-medium contractors wanting comprehensive features

**Pricing:**

**Weaknesses:**
- Expensive for small contractors

**ProjectTrackr Advantage:** 
- 1/3 the price
- Simpler UI (no overwhelm)
- Mobile included in all plans
- Longer trial period
